# Practical Fixes

This directory contains write-ups of practical troubleshooting scenarios involving common IT problems or unique one-off situations. These cases highlight real-world challenges where observation, intuition, or a creative approach was key to identifying and resolving the issue.

## What Makes These Cases Practical
Cases in this section typically involve one or more of the following:

- Common issues resolved through straightforward solutions  
- Situations where standard procedures required adaptation or creative application  
- Problems that were resolved efficiently, often without extensive diagnostic tools  
- Insights gained from resolving unusual or unexpected behavior  
- Lessons learned from hands-on experience and iterative troubleshooting  

## How to Read These Write-Ups
These write-ups focus on **observations, reasoning, and practical outcomes**, rather than exhaustive technical documentation. The goal is to provide a clear account of the troubleshooting process, practical solutions applied, and key takeaways for future reference, and potential tips and tricks.
